Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a brake job cost?

Brake pad replacement starts at $149 per axle. The final cost depends on your vehicle make/model and whether rotors need resurfacing or replacement. We provide free estimates before any work begins.

How long does an oil change take?

Most oil changes are completed in 30-45 minutes. We recommend making an appointment to minimize wait time, but we also accept walk-ins when available.

Do you offer a warranty on repairs?

Yes! We offer a 2-year/24,000-mile warranty on most repairs. Some parts may have manufacturer warranties that extend beyond this. Ask your service advisor for details.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, and check. We also offer financing options for larger repairs.

Do you provide a shuttle service?

Yes! We offer complimentary shuttle service within a 5-mile radius for customers who need transportation while their vehicle is being serviced.

How do I know if I need brake service?

Common signs include squeaking/grinding noises, vibrating brake pedal, brake warning light, car pulling to one side when braking, or a soft/spongy brake pedal. Get a free inspection if you notice any of these.
