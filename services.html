🛑 Brake Repair & Replacement

Your brakes are the most important safety system in your vehicle. Don't wait until it's too late.

Our Brake Services Include:

  • Complete brake inspection and diagnosis
  • Brake pad replacement (front and rear)
  • Brake rotor resurfacing or replacement
  • Brake caliper repair and replacement
  • Brake fluid flush and exchange
  • ABS system diagnostics and repair

Warning Signs You Need Brake Service:

  • ⚠️ Squeaking or grinding noises when braking
  • ⚠️ Vibrating or pulsating brake pedal
  • ⚠️ Brake warning light on dashboard
  • ⚠️ Car pulls to one side when braking
  • ⚠️ Soft or spongy brake pedal
